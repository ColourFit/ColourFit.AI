Privacy Policy

How ColorFit AI collects, uses, and protects your information

📅 Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Welcome to ColorFit AI ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at https://colourfit.github.io/ColourFit.AI/. By using our site, you agree to this policy.

📋 1. Information We Collect

ColorFit AI is a client-side web app. We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers. The following is processed only locally in your browser:

  • Age, height, weight, and gender (for outfit suggestions)
  • Skin tone HEX color code (optional)
  • Occasion preference

🔒 All inputs are processed only in your browser and are never sent to any server or stored anywhere.

🍪 2. Cookies & Tracking

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to show personalized ads based on your browsing history.

  • Google may use the DoubleClick cookie to serve ads
  • Third-party vendors may use cookies based on prior visits to our site
  • You can opt out at Google Ad Settings

📢 3. Google AdSense & Advertising

Our website uses Google AdSense (by Google LLC) to display relevant advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

  • Opt out of personalized ads at AboutAds.info
  • Learn how Google uses data: Google Privacy Policy
  • We do not control which ads are shown — Google determines this

📌 Ad content is determined by Google based on your browsing behavior and interests — not by ColorFit AI.

🔗 4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses these third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Unsplash — fashion images loaded from Unsplash servers
  • Google Fonts — for Syne & DM Sans typography
  • Google AdSense — for displaying advertisements

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

💾 5. Data Storage & Security

ColorFit AI has no backend server or database. All outfit suggestions, color analysis, and computations run entirely in your browser via JavaScript.

  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • No accounts or user profiles are created
  • No data is stored beyond your current browser session
  • Closing the tab clears all your inputs immediately

👶 6. Children's Privacy

ColorFit AI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used our site and shared personal information through third-party services, please contact us.

⚖️ 7. Your Rights

Since we don't store personal data, you have full control through browser settings:

  • Opt out of Google personalized ads via Ad Settings
  • Clear cookies from your browser at any time
  • Use private/incognito mode to limit tracking
  • Use ad-blocking extensions if you prefer
