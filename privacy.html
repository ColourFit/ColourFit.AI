Privacy Policy

How ColorFit AI handles your information

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Welcome to ColorFit AI. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at colourfitai.vercel.app/. By using our site, you agree to this policy.

1 Information We Collect

ColorFit AI is a client-side web application. We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers. The following is processed only locally in your browser:

  • Age, height, weight and gender (used for outfit suggestions)
  • Skin tone HEX color code (optional)
  • Occasion preference selected by you

All inputs are processed only in your browser and are never sent to any server or stored anywhere outside your device.

2 Cookies and Tracking

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to show personalized ads based on your browsing history.

  • Google may use the DoubleClick cookie to serve ads
  • Third-party vendors may use cookies based on your prior visits
  • You can opt out at Google Ad Settings

3 Google AdSense

Our website uses Google AdSense by Google LLC to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

  • Opt out of personalized ads at AboutAds.info
  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • We do not control which ads are shown

Ad content is determined by Google based on your browsing behavior — not by ColorFit AI.

4 Third-Party Services

Our website uses these third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Unsplash — fashion images from Unsplash servers
  • Google Fonts — Syne and DM Sans typography
  • Google AdSense — for displaying advertisements
  • GitHub Pages — for website hosting

5 Data Storage and Security

ColorFit AI has no backend server or database. All outfit suggestions, color analysis and computations run entirely in your browser via JavaScript.

  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • No accounts or user profiles are created
  • No data is stored beyond your current browser session
  • Closing the browser tab clears all your inputs immediately

6 Children's Privacy

ColorFit AI is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used our site, please contact us.

7 Your Rights

Since we do not store personal data, you have full control through your browser settings:

  • Opt out of Google personalized ads via Ad Settings
  • Clear cookies from your browser at any time
  • Use private or incognito mode to limit tracking
  • Use ad-blocking extensions if preferred

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the Last Updated date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
